## **📌 Overview**

The **Disruption Index** is a modular, open-source framework designed to quantify and analyze disruptions in various aspects of life. Whether you're tracking disruptions in your **home environment**, **personal well-being** (e.g., sleep, rest), or **professional productivity** (e.g., work, networking), this project provides a structured approach to measuring, visualizing, and mitigating disruptions.

### **Why It Matters**
Disruptions—intentional or unintentional—can significantly impact productivity, well-being, and quality of life. By systematically tracking these disruptions, individuals, organizations, and communities can:
- Identify patterns and root causes.
- Develop targeted interventions.
- Improve resilience and adaptability.

---

## **✨ Features**

### **📊 Core Framework**
- **Multi-Domain Support**: Track disruptions across **home, outdoors, personal well-being, and professional settings**.
- **Modular Design**: Customize indexes for specific use cases (e.g., sleep, work focus, networking).
- **Composite Indexes**: Aggregate scores for holistic insights (e.g., Overall Disruption Index, Intentional Disruption Index).

### **📈 Measurement & Analysis**
- **Frequency, Intensity, Duration**: Capture the full spectrum of disruption impact.
- **Weighted Scoring**: Prioritize subcategories based on importance.
- **Normalization**: Standardize metrics for comparability.

### **📉 Visualization & Reporting**
- **Dashboards**: Real-time tracking of disruption scores.
- **Heatmaps**: Identify high-disruption periods or areas.
- **Trends**: Time-series analysis for pattern recognition.

### **🛠️ Data Collection**
- **Manual Input**: Surveys and logs for subjective or qualitative data.
- **Automated Tracking**: Integrate with sensors, apps, or APIs for objective data (e.g., noise levels, sleep trackers).
- **Custom Metrics**: Define and add your own disruption metrics.

### **🔍 Use Cases**
- **Personal**: Monitor your own disruption levels to improve well-being.
- **Organizational**: Track employee productivity and workplace stability.
- **Societal**: Assess community or regional disruption trends.
